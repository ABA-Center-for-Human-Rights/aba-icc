The Office of the Prosecutor issued a public arrest warrant for Al-Senussi on June 27, 2011. On July 24, 2014 the Appeals Chamber [confirmed](https://www.icc-cpi.int/en_menus/icc/press%20and%20media/press%20releases/Pages/pr1034.aspx) Pre-Trial Chamber I's decision declaring the case against him inadmissible before the ICC.
