Pre-Trial Chamber II issued a sealed arrest warrant for Bemba on May 23, 2008, which was unsealed on May 24, 2008. Charges against him were confirmed on June 15, 2009. His trial ended on November 13, 2014, and was found guilty on April 21, 2016. He was sentenced, on June 21, 2016, to 18 years of imprisonment from which his time already spent in ICC custody will be deducted.
