The ICC Pre-Trial Chamber II [issued an arrest warrant under seal for Vincent Otti](https://www.icc-cpi.int/Pages/record.aspx?docNo=ICC-02/04-01/05-54)&nbsp;on July 8, 2005.&nbsp; The ICC Pre-Trial Chamber II [decided to unseal the arrest warrant](https://www.icc-cpi.int/Pages/record.aspx?docNo=ICC-02/04-01/15-34) on October 13, 2005.&nbsp; As of June 2016, the suspect remains at-large, but there are reports that the suspect is&nbsp;[deceased](http://www.haguejusticeportal.net/index.php?id=8194).
